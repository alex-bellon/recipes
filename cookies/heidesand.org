- ½ pound Butter (cannot be margarine)
- 1 cup Sugar
- 3 cups All-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Melt butter in a saucepan until lightly browned. Pour into mixing bowl and let cool.
3. Add sugar and beat until very well mixed. Add flour little by little while continuing to mix.
4. Make rolls about 1 inch in diameter and cut into ¼-inch slices.
5. Place slices on ungreased cookie sheet and bake for about 15 minutes until golden.
6. Yield: About 4 dozen cookies
